About Us

Who Are We?



Welcome to Mom and Pop, where every slice tells a story!

At Mom and Pop, we believe that pizza is more than just a dish—it's a tradition, a comfort, and a way of bringing people together. Our journey began in a small kitchen with a big dream: to create the most delicious, authentic pizzas that capture the essence of homemade cooking.

Family-owned and operated in Marietta, Mom and Pop is rooted in a passion for quality ingredients, time-honored recipes, and heartfelt hospitality. From hand-tossed crusts to carefully curated toppings, each pizza is crafted with love and dedication, just like Mama used to make.

But Mom and Pop is more than just a pizzeria; it's a place where memories are made, friendships are forged, and laughter fills the air. Whether you're sharing a pie with loved ones, savoring a quiet moment alone, or gathering with friends for a feast, our cozy atmosphere and friendly staff ensure that every visit feels like coming home.

So come on in, pull up a chair, and join us on a culinary adventure that's been generations in the making. From our family to yours, welcome to Mom and Pop—where every slice is made with love.
